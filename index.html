✒My failures as a Developer

✯Assuming the user and the environment

  • This is the worst thing one developer can do to deteriorate their career graph. A developer should never ever do this, as this could be the worst thing one can do as a developer. Developer should try and maintain a good level of communication with their client and create a website according to their requirements and needs.

    • ✯Not Being Detail Oriented

    • Not only Web development but even the smallest of tasks need proper detailing and sticking to those details always helps one out in carrying out all sort of tasks. A detail oriented person is never subjected to humiliation. Being a web developer if someone is not detail oriented then that one are putting themself in dire straits.

      • ✯Multitasking

      • Web development is a field which requires a lot of concentration and focus. One shouldn't risk it by Multitasking. One will certainly tend to ruin their career as a web developer even before it is started. So one should just focus on one proper goal and move ahead. Don't risk your career by multi-tasking. Avoid all the other distractions and just focus on your goal as this habit could be the reason for your failure as a web developer.

        • ✯Being a lone warrior

        • Working in collaboration is a must when you are a developer. Many budding developers quite often make this mistake by working in isolation and ruin their career by feeling that they have failed. One must always ask for help from their seniors as they have also gone through the same phase during their time. And also they should help others who needs their help. So, Kindly avoid this mistake and work in a healthy cum collaborative environment.
